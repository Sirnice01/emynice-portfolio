About Me

I TELL STORIES
WITH FRAMES

I'm Emmynice — a video creator with a deep passion for visual storytelling. I don't just shoot and edit footage; I craft experiences that resonate, that move people, that stay with them long after the screen goes dark.

My journey into video creation started with a simple camera and an obsession with capturing the world as I see it — raw, honest, and cinematic. Every project I take on is approached with the same question: what feeling do I want the viewer to walk away with?

From documentary and short-form content to brand campaigns and highlight reels — my style blends kinetic editing, intentional color grading, and a strong narrative sense. If you have a story, I have the frame for it.
